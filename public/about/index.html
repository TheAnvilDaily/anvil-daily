About The Anvil Daily

Independent journalism that checks the math.

The Anvil Daily is a conservative news aggregator built on a simple premise: the most important stories of the day deserve more than a headline. We pull from the top conservative media outlets every hour, surface the stories that matter, and add original commentary explaining why each story is significant for everyday Americans.

We do not spin. We do not editorialize for clicks. We hit hard on the facts, give you the context the mainstream press leaves out, and let you decide.

What We Do

Every hour, our editorial system scans the top conservative news sources for the day's most consequential reporting. We surface the top stories, link directly to the original reporting, and publish original "Why This Matters" commentary -- a plain-English breakdown of each story's real-world implications for America.

We are an aggregator, not a publisher. Every story links back to its original source. We credit every outlet.
