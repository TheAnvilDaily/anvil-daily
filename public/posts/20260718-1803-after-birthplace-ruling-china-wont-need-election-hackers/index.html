# Why This Matters

Their allegiances, their families, their entire support network might be overseas. And we're handing them ballots. It's not racist to notice the pattern; it's suicidal to ignore it.

Real Americans didn't sign up for this. We didn't win the Cold War just to hand the keys to the kingdom to the highest bidder. Fix this now, or watch your vote become as worthless as the ink it's printed with.
